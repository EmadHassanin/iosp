To restore the overriden colours you can redefine them in the yaml header.

title: "IOSlides custom box colours"
output:
  iosp::ioslides_plus:
    box_colours:
      red: red
      green: green
      blue: blue
      yellow: yellow
      gray: gray
      white: white
